學院簡介

中國科技大學規劃與設計學院歡迎您的加入

Welcome to join us

中國科技大學規劃與設計學院歡迎您的加入！本規設院設有建築系暨碩士班、土木與防災設計系暨碩士班、室內設計系暨碩士班、視覺傳達設計系暨碩士班、數位多媒體設計系、影視設計系及學士後數位音樂音效學位學程共六系+一學程，各系的教學設計皆以學生生涯規劃為導向，教學目標融合了設計、藝術、創意美學和科技，理論與實務並重。並因應社會多元化之需求，利用各種專業教室模擬實習環境，並安排校外學習機會，加強學生就業適應能力。

學院特色

規劃設計到營造/數位影音到新媒體/技術專精到多元專長。

教育目標

因應21世紀網路與數位科技所發展的環境空間與規劃、數位傳播、文化創意、娛樂產業、視覺設計、與數位學習等「數位內容」產業的發展趨勢需求，以培育全方位跨領域之整合型專業人才，及具國際觀之未來設計師為教育目標。

Education Goals

Education goals of our college are making designers with integrated professional skills and global perspectives. In order to satisfy the trend of digital contain development with space and environments, digital broadcasting, cultural creativity, entertainment, visual design and e-learning in the 21st century.

學院發展

本院為培育全方位專業人才，將研擬規劃相關學程，或訂定院共同必修科目，協助學生擴展學習領域，厚植科技專業基礎。 倡導共用教學設備、教學空間與實驗儀器，有效整合與運用資源，協助各系所發展重點與特色，並提供師生優質之學習及研究環境。 發展教師橫向跨系所的合作與整合，建立系所內教學、研究、服務等方面之專業團隊，進而擴展成為跨系所、甚至跨院之研究群。進而成立專業之研發中心，配合各實驗室之儀器設備，透過本校研究發展處，拓展服務層面，提供推廣與產業服務。

The college cultivates professional persons with relative programs to extend their learning field and establish the solid foundation of technology as well. 提供學生完善的設備及實用的專業知識，培育具有設計規畫、執行實務所需技術及使用工具之專業能力，可積極主動發掘、分析及處理問題。加強人文素養，並注重溝通與團隊合作能力的培養，期能瞭解專業倫理及社會責任，以因應職場之競爭。

建築系/學制：日四技、進四技、進院二技、碩士班

Department/Graduate Institute of Architecture / 4-year undergraduate course (daytime) / 4-year continuing education course / 2-year continuing education course / graduate level course

是培育建築生命週期各業一流人才的基地；每年建築師錄取人數，本系系友高達12%以上。

The department is a base for cultivating first-class talents in various fields within architectural design. Each year as many as 12% of applying architects are enrolled.

系特色


本系以培育「建築生命週期」相關專業人才為主，除建築計畫、資訊、美學、手繪等技能外，課程分「建築規劃與設計實務」及「建築工程與科技實務」二模組，內含環境規劃與設計、歷史環境保存與再利用、都市更新與物業管理、建築設備與環境控制、營建施工與構造及建築數位技術與系統運用(BIM)等五項專業特色職能。藉由專業課程、實習及產學合作等實務操作培育職場需求之能力及技術，以達成「畢業即可高薪就業」的教育目標。專業與生活品格陶冶及人文與環境關懷並重，與建築師公會及著名營建相關企業集團有非常密切的夥伴關係，每年亦提供甚多之實習機會與獎學金及協助專業課程建構與協同授課。近幾年系友考取建築師佔該年全國錄取總數之12%以上，畢業系友表現普獲業界好評與器重。
本系碩士班招收以培育「永續環境」之環境規劃與設計、歷史環境保存與再利用、都市更新與物業管理、建築設備與環境控制、營建施工與構造、建築數位技術與系統運用(BIM)等六項專業職能特色之研究與應用性人才為主。強調理論結合實務之學習，並與規劃設計學院相關系所整合，使學生畢業後具備邏輯、分析與應用能力，提升職場上的競爭力。

系所招生特色網址：https://www.cute.edu.tw/arch/

The department is primarily involved in cultivating professional talent in the 'architectural life cycle'. Apart from blueprints, information, aesthetics, drafting, and other technical skills, the courses are divided into two modules: 'architectural planning and design practice' and 'architectural engineering and technology practice'. These cover the professional skills of environmental planning and design, historical preservation and environmental reuse, urban renewal and property management, architectural equipment and environmental control, construction sites and structures, as well as digital construction technology and system applications (BIM). Through these professional-level courses, internships, and industry-academia cooperation, the department can cultivate the skills needed in the workplace and reach the educational goal of high-paying employment upon graduation. Ensuring professionalism and quality of life share an important position in both the humanities and environmental care. Having very close partnerships with the Architects Association as well as major construction groups and architecture-related companies, every year there are numerous internship opportunities, scholarships, and other assistance provided in the construction and co-teaching of professional-level courses. In recent years, department alumni have proved the department's positive evaluations and high status in the industry, with alumni now accounting for over 12% of the nationwide total number of architects registered each year.
The master's program is oriented towards developing talent through research and application of six professional skill areas, including environmental planning and design, historical preservation and environmental reuse, urban renewal and property management, architectural equipment and environmental control, construction sites and structures, and digital construction technology and system applications (BIM), all under the umbrella of environmental sustainability. In emphasising the combination of theory and practice, the program is integrated with other related departments in the College of Planning and Design, allowing students to prepare the logical, analytic, and applied skills that will increase their employability in a competitive job market.

Department Website: https://www.cute.edu.tw/arch/
